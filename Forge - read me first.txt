FORGE — Trading Journal (standalone app)
========================================

HOW TO RUN
1. Double-click  Forge.exe
2. A black window opens (that's the app running — leave it open).
3. Your browser opens to the journal automatically.
   If it doesn't, open your browser and go to:  http://localhost:8000
4. To stop Forge, close the black window.

That's it — no install, no Docker, no Python needed.

FIRST TIME: WINDOWS SMARTSCREEN
The app isn't code-signed, so Windows may show a blue
"Windows protected your PC" box. This is normal for any new .exe.
Click "More info" -> "Run anyway".

YOUR DATA
Everything you enter (accounts, trades, notes, uploads) is stored
privately on THIS computer at:
   C:\Users\<you>\AppData\Local\Forge
It is not sent anywhere. Back it up by copying that folder.

IMPORTING TRADES
On the Upload page, drop a Sierra Chart Trade Activity Log (.txt)
or a NinjaTrader Executions export (.csv). Accounts are created
automatically; apply a prop-firm preset on the Prop Firms page.

NOTES
- Windows 64-bit only.
- This is a personal/local app: each person who runs it has their
  own separate journal and data. It is not a shared website.
